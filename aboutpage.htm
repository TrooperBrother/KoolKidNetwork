Why was the Kool Kid Network made?

To provide a place for kids to make games in Wick editor and share them on the site, and to share movies that people make. Because it is a smaller community you could get a good amount of feedback.



What type of content will be hosted on this site?

Small point-and-click games that are school-friendly and movies that are school friendly, if you have any questions about what you may or may not find on this site please email us at jkstanw09@gmail.com.
